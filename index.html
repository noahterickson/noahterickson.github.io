Welcome!

Hello! My name is Noah Erickson and you have reached my personal website. I threw this website together to host my blog but you can also find my resume and contact information here.

My blog's current purpose is to collect my thoughts and summaries about whatever book I am currently reading. In the future I may expand the blogs scope to include my family's activities I also intend on adding a gallery where you can find photos of projects going on around my family's homestead and of course photos of our pets.

Professionally, I am an embedded systems engineer working on a small team that designs battery powered power tools. I graduated from Portland State University in 2016 with a degree in electrical engineering and have resided in the Portland metro area my whole life.
